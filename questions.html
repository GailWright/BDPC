Can I simply have my employees clean-up for me?

No.  Business owners can not let their employees clean up after any suicide or accidental death.  Anyone cleaning up a potentially toxic site must follow Federal Regulation 29CFR1910.1030, which maintains that no individual can be exposed to blood spills without:

-Receiving blood-borne pathogen training

-A written blood-borne pathogen control plan

-Use of personal protective equipment

-Having been offered Hepatitis B vaccine protection
